Pesto alla Genovese recipe

Home

This famous genovese dish is very simply to cook, let's see together how to prepare it!

Ingredients:

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Parmigiano reggiano cheese
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Garlic
  • Pine nuts
  • Salt

How to do:

  1. Let's start with the garlic, crush it in a mortar until smooth
  2. Add the basil leaves and gently crush them in the mortar to release their aroma
  3. Add pine nuts and cruch them as well
  4. Pour in a generouse amount of olive oil
  5. Add Parmigiano and pecorino cheese
  6. Continue crushing and mixing everything together until the mixture becomes thick and well-combined
  7. Serve with your favourite type of pasta and enjoy!
