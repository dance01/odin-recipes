This famous italian dish is very simply to cook, let's see together how to make it!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta sfoglia
  • Besciamella
  • Ragù
  • Parmigiano

How to do:

  1. In a baking pan, spread a layer of ragù
  2. Above it put a layer of besciamella
  3. cover with a layer of pasta sfoglia
  4. Repeat the process until you reach the top of the pan
  5. At the end sprinkle a lovely snowfall of Parmigiano Reggiano
  6. Place pan inside the oven at 120 degree for 20 minutes
  7. Serve and Enjoy!
